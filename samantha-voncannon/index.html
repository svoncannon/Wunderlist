Welcome to Wunderlist 2.0

Stop forgetting to do what you need to do.

Buried Under Tasks?

Ever feel like your week is just a series of to-dos? Endless tasks needing to be tracked and get done?

We feel your pain. It's why we wanted to create the world's best task manager - something that is as light and fun as it is powerful and effective.

The Wunder Way

Wunderlist 2.0 takes your traditional to-do list and automates it. It not only allows you to write your to-do, work, grocery, trip, and household lists, but allows you to set up recurring to do lists by date and time.

No more pen and paper. The app goes with you wherever you take your device.

Here's How it Works

Step 1

Simply add your task from your phone or on your desktop.

Step 2

Manage your lists, checking them off as you go.

Step 3

Get back to the things that matter, with your tasks tackled.

No matter how much is on your plate, Wunderlist 2.0 makes it super easy to remember all the little recurring to-dos and surprise to-dos that pop up unexpectedly.
